Featured Project

OneHealth Hub & the CCOHS framework

Self-directed · Human–Animal–Environmental–Zoonotic

A surveillance platform built from a framework I designed myself.

I created the Community-Centred One Health Surveillance (CCOHS) framework — a five-pillar model for capturing event-based community signals of zoonotic spillovers — and then built its proof-of-concept, OneHealth Hub, end-to-end from zero prior full-stack experience.

Benchmarked against DHIS2, SORMAS, and AfyaData. A manuscript detailing the framework is in preparation for One Health (Elsevier).
