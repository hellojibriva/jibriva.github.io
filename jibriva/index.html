Common questions

How much does a restaurant website cost?

Starter package is ₦40,000 for a single-page site with menu, WhatsApp ordering, and SEO. Business package is ₦80,000 for up to 5 pages plus Google Business Profile setup, gallery, and Google Maps. Need booking or online ordering? I'll quote a Premium package.

How long does it take?

Most restaurant websites are ready within 3–5 working days after I receive your content (menu, photos, location details). Revisions typically add 2–3 more days.

Can I update my menu myself?

Yes. I'll show you how to make simple updates. For bigger changes like adding a whole new section, I offer affordable monthly maintenance plans.

Do I need to buy a domain name?

I recommend it — a custom domain like yourrestaurant.com.ng looks more professional and helps with Google ranking. I can help you buy and set it up.

Will my site work on phones?

Absolutely. Every site I build is mobile-first — meaning I design for phones first, then adapt for tablets and desktops. Your customers will get a fast, clean experience on any device.
